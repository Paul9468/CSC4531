This Web App fetches and presents data from the World of Warcraft Auction House API.


Head over to the "Recipes" tab and start creating new recipes by hitting "New Recipe".

Then, your recipes will be listed in the "Recipes" page and their profit will be evaluated.


You can enter a username to seperate your recipes, or keep it by default.

Region and Server are defaulted to EU:Archimonde and cannot be changed at this time.
